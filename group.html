APS Personality and Individual Differences Psychology Interest Group

The Australian Psychological Society provides the opportunity people to develop and run interest groups related to the discipline and practice of psychology.

Many of our conference attendees are also members of the Interest Group. It is through the APS and the Interest Group that we are able to manage the finances of the group and the conference.

You do not have to be a member of the APS to be a member of the Interest Group. More information can be found at the Interest Group website.
